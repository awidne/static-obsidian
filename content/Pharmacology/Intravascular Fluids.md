# Intravascular Fluids
## Background
Under normal physiological conditions, renal function regulates volume, osmolality, and electrolyte balance. Normal minimum daily water intake (from all sources) is approximately 1600 mL with an equivalent output. 

In inpatient care, fluid replenishment is often required in cases of fluid losses. This is individualized as various conditions change fluid needs/losses and is further impacted by third-spacing.
