# Jack's Med Notes

This is a repository of condensed notes organized by topic as a means of consolidating the scattered notes of a *spiral curriculum*.

Many pages are currently work-in-progress. I only update the notes when I feel like it.

**Disclaimer**: the purpose of these notes is for my personal learning. They are hosted online for my own ease of access and are **not** intended to be used as a primary resource for studying or patient care.
## Sources
The notes taken are a combination of lecture material, notes from group discussion, clinical pearls from placements, and research. As such, an exhaustive source list is impossible.

Some resources used for these notes include:
