# Massive Transfusion Protocol
## Overview
Massive transfusion refers to either:
- Replacement of >1 blood volume in 24 hours
- >50% of blood volume in 4 hours (approximately 70 mL/kg in adult)
- Transfusion of >40 mL/kg in children 
## Indications
A massive transfusion protocol should be used
